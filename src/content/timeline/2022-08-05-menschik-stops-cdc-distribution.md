In response to an FDA official's question about sending COVID-19 vaccine data mining to CDC, Menschik confirms to his FDA colleagues, "[W]e are no longer routinely sending COVID data mining to CDC[.]"[^fn-223] Menschik notes that Nair had previously discussed this decision with CDC official Shimabukuro.[^fn-224]
