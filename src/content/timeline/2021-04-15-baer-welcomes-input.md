- Baer responds, stating, "I think we should welcome any expert input," acknowledging that she and Menschik "are concerned about the effect of so many COVID reports on the standard system [FDA] use[s]."[^fn-134] She asks if there is any way to have DuMouchel more involved in FDA's "data mining process and interpretation during this unprecedented reporting time?"[^fn-135]
- Niu answers stating she will send them DuMouchel's data mining results and that "[t]he best person to ask would be Ana [Szarfman] as she has close ties with Bill DuMouchel."[^fn-136]
- A few minutes later, Menschik removes Niu from the email chain and replies only to Zinderman and Baer, pushing back on immediately speaking with Szarfman: "Before we potentially reach out to Ana [Szarfman], we should meet internally – many considerations not suited to email…"[^fn-137] It is unclear if Menschik, Zinderman, and Baer met.
