Following her July 9 phone call, Szarfman writes Forshee, "As we talked over the phone, I became aware last Fri[day] that scientists from Cornell are concerned of an increased mortality signal with the COVID-19 vaccines."[^fn-175] Szarfman encloses DuMouchel's earlier RGPS analysis, showing statistically significant safety signals for "death and sudden death" associated with the Pfizer and Moderna COVID-19 vaccines.[^fn-176] She writes that the method DuMouchel used "automatically unmask[s] signals that remain hidden by other data mining methodologies, including MGPS[,]" FDA's current data mining methodology.[^fn-177]
