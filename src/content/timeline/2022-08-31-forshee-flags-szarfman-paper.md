Forshee writes to Marks and Anderson, notifying them that Szarfman is a co-author on "Signaling COVID-19 Vaccine Adverse Events."[^fn-225] He references that they are discussing best ways to address "a number of issues with the paper and its findings[.]"[^fn-226] Forshee references Szarfman's earlier efforts in 2021 raising concerns about FDA's current data mining system.[^fn-227]
