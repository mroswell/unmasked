Szarfman emails her paper "Signaling COVID-19 Vaccine Adverse Events" to then-FDA Commissioner Robert Califf, noting that it describes "advances in the data mining (DM) methodology, especially the capacity to unmask hidden signals due to previously unaccountable confounders[.]"[^fn-221] Upon receipt of this and another article, Califf replies, "Thanks. These are good."[^fn-222]
